Privacy

Wayfinder is a small tool, and this is a short, plain policy to match.

What you type

When you look up a verse or describe what's going on in "Start From How You're Feeling," that text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate an explanation or a guided path. It is not stored in a database, not linked to your identity, and not used to build a profile of you. There are no accounts and no login.

Short-term caching

To keep the app fast and affordable to run, explanations for common Bible verses and previously-asked situational prompts are cached in the server's memory for reuse. This cache resets whenever the server restarts and is not a permanent record — it exists purely to avoid asking the AI the same question twice.

What stays on your device

If Wayfinder shows a "History" of verses you've viewed, that list is stored only in your browser's local storage — it never leaves your device, and clearing it or clearing your browser data removes it completely.

Third parties involved

Two external services are used to make Wayfinder work: Anthropic (AI explanations) and bible-api.com (public-domain scripture text for verses outside the curated library). Neither receives any information beyond the specific verse reference or text you're asking about.

No ads, no tracking, no selling data

There is no advertising in Wayfinder, no analytics tracking your behavior, and nothing about your usage is sold or shared for marketing purposes.

Questions

This app is run by Core Covenant. If you have a question about how it handles information, reach out through Core Covenant's usual contact channels.
